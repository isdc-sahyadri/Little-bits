Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How can I register a complaint?

You can register a complaint by navigating to the "Register Complaint" section in the dashboard and filling out the required details.

❓ How do I track my complaint status?

Go to the "Track Complaint" section in the dashboard and enter your complaint ID to see the current status.

❓ Can I edit or delete my complaint?

No, once a complaint is submitted, it cannot be deleted. However, you can update additional information by contacting support.

❓ What happens after I submit a complaint?

After submission, your complaint is reviewed by the relevant department, and you will receive updates via email or SMS.

❓ How do I contact support?

You can contact support via the "Help" section in your dashboard or email us at support@smartcityhub.com.
