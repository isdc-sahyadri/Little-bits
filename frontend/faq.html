FAQS

Registering a complaint is simple. Navigate to the **"Register Complaint"** section in your dashboard. You will need to fill in the details such as **complaint category, description, location, and supporting documents (if any).** Once submitted, a unique **Complaint ID** will be generated. Keep this ID for tracking your complaint status.

To track your complaint, visit the **"Track Complaint"** section in the dashboard. Enter your **Complaint ID** and click **Search**. You will see real-time updates, including whether the complaint is **Pending, In Progress, or Resolved.** Notifications will also be sent via **email and SMS**.

Once a complaint is submitted, **deleting** it is not possible to maintain transparency. However, if you have additional details or corrections, you can **edit the complaint within 24 hours** of submission. After that, any modifications require contacting the **support team** via the "Help" section.

Once you submit a complaint, it is assigned to the **relevant department** for review. A city official will verify the details, and based on urgency, it will be categorized as **High, Medium, or Low Priority**. You will receive updates when an official is assigned and when **action is taken.** If needed, you may be contacted for further details.
